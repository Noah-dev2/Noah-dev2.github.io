Brisket

History

Jewish immigrants brought their brisket traditions to America in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, where the availability of beef made it more accessible

This recipe makes a delicious and tender Brisket. This recipe is really easy as it doesnt take much work and it doesnt require many steps.

Ingredients

  • 4-6 pounds of beef brisket, visible fat untrimmed
  • one(12-ounce) can of Coca-Cola
  • 1 cup bottled chili sauce
  • 1 envelope dried onion soup mix

Directions

  1. preheat the oven to 325F
  2. pat the brisket dry with paper towels. Place brisket in roasting pan/dish that perfectly fits the brisket.
  3. Mix the Coca-Cola, 1 cup bottle chili sauce, and onion soup mix in a bowl and pour over brisket
    Roast the brisket until tender, about 30 minutes per pound.
  4. Transer brisket to cutting board and let it rest for 10 minutes.
    Meanwhile, skim the fat from the surfac of the pan juices into a gravy boat.
  5. Trim any visible fat from the brisket, then thinly slice it against the grain.
    Pile slices of brisket on a platter and pass the warm pan juices on the side.
